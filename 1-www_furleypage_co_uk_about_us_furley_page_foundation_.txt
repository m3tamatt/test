Foundation Furley Page Foundation Furley Page Foundation is a registered charity responsible for managing all social responsibility programmes of Furley Page Solicitors, which includes fundraising for local good causes and charities.
The Foundation has a Board of Trustees which is made up of a mix of Partners and elected staff representatives from within Furley Page. In this section About us Our culture Our values History of Furley Page Organisations supported by Furley Page Furley Page Foundation Furley Page Foundation grant making policy Donate to Furley Page Foundation Careers Funds are available for local good causes and charities, the distribution of those funds will be decided by the trustees.
Every year Furley Page staff nominate a main charity which is supported by funding raising activities. Kent and Medway is where most Furley Page staff live and work, which is why we participate in community initiatives and support local charities through the Furley Page Foundation.
Furley Page Foundation is a Charitable Incorporated Organisation (CIO) registered with the Charity Commission – Registered Charity number 1163007.
