property rights Intellectual property rights Intellectual property rights – sometimes referred to as IP or IPR – can play a vital role in your business. That’s why understanding, protecting and utilising your intellectual property is crucial to your continued success.
We advise businesses at all stages of the business life cycle from entrepreneurs, to start-ups looking to protect their intellectual property.
If you are a victim of infringement or find yourself in a dispute about intellectual property, we will guide you through the IPR considerations in your commercial contracts and we can help you to understand your options.
