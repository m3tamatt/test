CT5 1BG Telephone 01227 274241 Fax 01227 275704 DX 32352 Whitstable Email the office Map and Directions From the A299 Whitstable roundabout follow main route into Whitstable High Street, approximately 2 miles, enter High Street. Proceed until you reach a Y junction towards the far end of the High Street.
You will need to park in this area, there are various public car parks signposted.
Whitstable Office is situated in this part of the High Street, opposite HSBC Bank.
Where to park in Whitstable
Whitstable has a number of public car parks which are signposted.
For up to date information about parking in Whitstable, visit Canterbury city council website and search, ‘Find a car park’ https://www.canterbury.gov.uk/ Solicitors in Whitstable
Furley Page is a leading law firm based in Kent. Our solicitors provide legal advice and exceptional service to both individuals and business clients in Whitstable and the surrounding areas. We also have offices located in Chatham and Canterbury.
Whitstable is a fishing and harbour town, famous for its oysters and quaint architecture. The Furley Page office can be easily found in Whitstable High Street. Our distinctive building has ground floor meeting rooms making it accessible for wheelchair users and clients with difficulties walking.
Our Whitstable legal teams provide services to individuals which include advice on Wills and probate, elderly and vulnerable client matters, Powers of Attorney and residential property; as well as offering commercial legal advice to local businesses.
The full range of our legal expertise is available to Whitstable based clients because of the close proximity to our Canterbury head office and the fact that all our offices interconnect seamlessly.
Coastal locations
Furley Page Whitstable office is within easy reach of East Kent coastal locations. We service clients from all around the East Kent coast including Herne Bay, Birchington, Westgate-on-Sea, Margate, Broadstairs and Ramsgate to name but a few.
