Our team of conveyancing solicitors have extensive knowledge of the local property market. We also maintain close relationships with other professionals within the property sector, both locally in Kent and throughout the country. What’s more, there are no hidden charges – we are completely transparent when we provide our clients with estimates for work.
We are proud to be an accredited member of the Law Society’s Conveyancing Quality Scheme (CQS). Our Conveyancing Quality Scheme accreditation means we are on the panel of all major lenders and smaller lenders of mortgages.
Our legal advisers are supported by a specialist real estate team which is recognised by The Legal 500 and Chambers UK – independent guides to the legal profession.
