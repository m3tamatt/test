Dentists The prospect of buying or selling a dental practice or starting your own business can be extremely daunting. We have years of experience acting for dentists on a wide range of commercial matters, in particular specialising in the sale and purchase of dental practices.
We have completed many dental transactions and understand how your business operates, as well as the complexities of an NHS contract and the Care Quality Commission (CQC) process. Legal advice for dentists
We frequently advise members of the dental profession on the following: The sale and purchase of dental practices, including dental body corporates
Mergers and restructuring of existing dental businesses
Setting up a new practice
Care Quality Commission (CQC) process and compliance
Dental Regulations
Partnership Agreements and Expense Sharing arrangements
Commercial Agreements
Associate and Hygienist Agreements
Disputes
Employment issues
Taking or moving premises Why choose Furley Page for dental sector advice
We understand the nuances of the dental sector and your business. Through our expertise and relationships with other dental professionals, we can offer relevant legal support and will successfully lead you through buying or selling a practice which could otherwise be a very stressful and drawn out process.
