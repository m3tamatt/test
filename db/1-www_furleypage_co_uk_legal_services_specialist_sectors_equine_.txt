Equine Owning a horse has always been a significant investment of both time and money.
Transactions used to be done on trust, however, in today’s litigious climate it is now absolutely imperative that you make sure that you are protected should things go wrong.
We are experienced in advising owners, purchasers and trainers on the legal considerations that should be born in mind when dealing with horses. Equine services Equine Equine agreements Bringing and defending equine claims Equine legal needs
Obtaining legal advice at the earliest opportunity will provide you with peace of mind regarding your rights and obligations and those of the parties that you are dealing with. This will help to avoid protracted and expensive disputes, as contracting parties you will know from the outset what each have agreed to.
