Legal specialists, exceptional service
Each of our lawyers is a specialist in their field of law which means you can be confident in the advice we provide.
Our expertise and exceptional service is acknowledged through independent client research carried out by The Legal 500, Chambers UK and Chambers HNW every year.

Our locations and our reach
We have offices in Canterbury and Whitstable in East Kent and in Chatham, Medway towards North and West Kent.
Our solicitors help clients across the county including all the main towns such as Maidstone, Ashford and down to the coast, like Margate and Dover.
With easy access to London, our lawyers service clients across the South East. Our French property and cross-border estates legal teams help people in France and other EU countries. Virtual appointments mean our solicitors and lawyers can reach and help clients almost anywhere if appropriate.
Quality and value
We offer city-level legal expertise, outstanding client service and we are known for our straight forward, pragmatic approach.
Tailored to you
Our lawyers listen to you to understand your legal requirements. We provide you with clear legal advice tailored specifically to your needs, which can range from fixed fee interviews to bespoke services.
