work placement scheme Summer work placement scheme Summer work placement scheme for graduates and undergraduates
Work placements can be the first step into a career within the legal sector. Furley Page wants to offer both Undergraduates and Graduates a meaningful work placement which provides our applicants with a clear insight into the workings of a large regional firm of solicitors.
We are offering up to 8 applicants the opportunity to undertake a one week summer placement with us. The application process for 2023 summer work placements is NOW CLOSED. Please visit our website later in the year for details of our 2024 summer work placement scheme. This year our one week summer placements will take place during one of the following two weeks: 12 June 2023
19 June 2023 Successful applicants will, over the course of the week, spend one day in each of our participating teams including: Private Client
Real Estate
Dispute Resolution
Personal Injury Throughout the week you will be partnered with our current Trainees to act as buddies who will assist you with any questions you may have.
Who should apply for the summer work placement scheme?
Client focused and commercially aware Undergraduates who have completed at least year one of their degree; or Graduates who have completed their degrees, regardless of the discipline and are looking for a career within the legal sector.
Graduates must have been awarded a 2:1 as a minimum.
