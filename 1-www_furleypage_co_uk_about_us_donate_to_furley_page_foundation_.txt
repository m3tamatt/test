Page Furley Page Foundation Furley Page Foundation grant making policy Donate to Furley Page Foundation Careers Donations
Furley Page can accept payments into Furley Page Foundation by Cheque, Bank Transfer or Standing Order. If you wish to make a financial donation to Furley Page Foundation please contact Lorraine Bunting on 01227 763939 who will help you through the payment process.
Gift Aid
If you are a UK tax payer your donation may eligible for Gift Aid. Please contact Ian Gilmour on 01227 763939 for advice on Gift Aid and tax.
Remember Furley Page Foundation in your will
If you make a will with Furley Page and would like to include a donation to Furley Page Foundation, please speak to Aaron Spencer on 01227 763939 to find out how this can be arranged.
