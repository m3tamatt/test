celebrates return of the East Kent Ploughing Match Furley Page celebrates return of the East Kent Ploughing Match Sarah Webster Partner & Head of Agriculture & Rural Business sw@furleypage.co.uk 01227 763939 View bio September 13, 2023 Furley Page, one of Kent’s leading law firms known for its expertise in agriculture and rural business, will attend the 78th East Kent Ploughing Match, on Wednesday 27 September 2023, at Hardres Court Farm in Canterbury.
This popular and eagerly anticipated event promises to be a showcase of both vintage and contemporary agricultural practices, with ploughing competitions, events and activities making it an exciting and educational day out for visitors.
Members of Furley Page’s Agriculture and Rural team will be available to speak to clients and visitors in the Furley Page marquee throughout the day.
Sarah Webster, Partner and Head of Agriculture and Rural Business at Furley Page, said: “The East Kent Ploughing Match is a mainstay of Kent’s agricultural scene and serves to bring the county’s rural community together. We eagerly look forward to catching up with our clients, friends and contacts at our stand throughout the day.”
The East Kent Ploughing Match has long been a highlight of Kent’s agricultural calendar, offering a delightful day out for families and a unique opportunity for networking and socialising while gaining insights into the world of agriculture.
Furley Page’s highly regarded Agriculture and Rural Business team is recommended by independent legal guides, The Legal 500 and Chambers UK.
