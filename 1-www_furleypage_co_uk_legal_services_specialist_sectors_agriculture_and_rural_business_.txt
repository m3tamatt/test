Agriculture and rural business Agriculture and rural business Having acted for farmers and rural land owners for generations, we have gained extensive insight into their special requirements.
We provide solutions to assist our clients in meeting today’s complexities and challenges in the agricultural sector. This includes the sale, purchase, and letting of farmland, as well as the property rights and interests that are incidental to land ownership. We advise farm businesses diversifying from pure agricultural production into other areas including farm retail, food and drink processing and provision of equine and other leisure facilities.
We have considerable experience in planning and land development schemes particularly those involving joint ventures between one or more landowners.

Farm Business Tenancies and Agricultural Holdings Act tenancies – new and on change
Agricultural and other rural support schemes
Business structures (partnerships & companies)
Joint venture, promotion agreements, options & conditional contracts
Contract and share farming
Business sales, acquisitions and mergers
Marketing and distribution agreements
Environmental and planning controls

Health & Safety legislation
Employment issues including gangmaster legislation
