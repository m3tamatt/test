Commercial property disputes between landlords and tenants: Relating to business lease renewals,
rent reviews,
breach of covenant by the landlord or the tenant,
forfeiture of leases,
dilapidations,
rent arrears and insolvency. We act for a wide range of landlords of students.
Agricultural property disputes: Including agricultural holdings,
farm business tenancies,
